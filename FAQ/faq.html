Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Max?


Max is a completely free online playground that offers a super fun experience with the best online games selection. You can play alone or with friends. We offer instant play for all of our games without needing to download, log in, see pop-ups, or deal with any other distractions. You can play our games on your desktop, tablet, and mobile so you can enjoy them at home, on the road, or while studying. Max has a large selection of Online Games; here are some of our top categories: 2 Player Games, .io Games, Car Games, and many more. We also have a bunch of Popular Games such as Subway Surfers, Stickman Hook, Pop It Master, Temple Run, and more.These games are created by web developers from all around the world who we work with closely to bring you the best play experience. Before releasing a game on our website, our Max team reviews them through a strict and careful selection process in order to ensure quality and a fun experience for all of our players.

Are Max games educational?


We have games of every kind! From Car Games to 2 Player Games, Dress Up Games to Adventure Games, and many more. However, if you’re looking for educational games only, look around in our Educational Games, Math Games, Multiplication Games, and Thinking Games.

Is Max safe and secure?


Yes, the safety of our users is extremely important to us. Max takes all necessary precautions and security measures to help ensure your safety and that of other players. Furthermore, every game on our platform has gone through a thorough quality assurance process. However, we can only guarantee your safety on our website. If you decide to leave our website through external links, we cannot guarantee your safety. Max runs completely in your browser, which is a safe fenced-off environment within your computer. Without permission, it is not possible to access files or install software on your device. For more information, we advise you to read our Terms of Use.

What can I do if I see an inappropriate ad?


The advertisements on our website are restricted to appropriate content only. That means we do our best to prevent and remove any content that is defamatory, indecent, obscene, harassing, violent, or otherwise objectionable material. If you nevertheless come across any inappropriate content, please let us know by sending an email to: hello@max.com. Any screenshots, links to the concerning advertisement, and other relevant information is also appreciated. This will help us speed up the process of finding and restricting these advertisements.

Can you get viruses on Max?


No, Max runs completely in your browser and we never ask you to download anything. It is therefore virtually impossible to get viruses from playing Max in-browser games. However, there are some websites out there that pretend to be Max (they steal our design, try to look like our homepage, use the same colors, etc.) . We cannot guarantee your safety on other websites. If you come across such websites, please do not hesitate to contact us at: hello@max.com.

Do I have to download or install something to play?


Never! You only need a web browser to play our games. We don’t ask you to download any files or programs onto your devices. Therefore, Max is a safe environment without the risk of getting a virus.

Does Max have a mobile app?


Currently, Max has no mobile app.

Are Max games completely free?


Yes. Every game is 100% free on Max! In order to keep our games completely free, we work with advertisers and share the revenue we make with the developers of the games available on Max.

Can you create an account on Max Websites?


No, you cannot create an account or login into our websites. This also means we in principle don’t ask for any personal information such as email address, phone number, credit card details, or any other information. You can play our games instantly with no hassle!

Does Max collect your data?


We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. All information on privacy and how we use data can be found under our Privacy Statement and our Cookie Statement. These statements are applicable to all Max domains - see the full list of our domains here.
